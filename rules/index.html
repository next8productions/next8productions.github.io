List operation

Our top 10 list is updated regularly to reflect the most popular and relevant sites. Below, we explain how the process works:

Loss of Positions

The sites on our list may lose positions for the following reasons:

  • When a site buys a position, the site that previously held it loses a position.
  • When a site moves down one position, the site that previously occupied it moves down one position.

list output

A site may be removed from the list for the following reasons:

  • When a site falls below the tenth position it is removed from the list.
  • Violation of our content policies.
